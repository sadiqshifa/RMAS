Methodology & sourcing notes

  • This tool contains no AI. Every determination is produced by fixed if/then logic against the inputs below. That is a deliberate design choice, not a limitation — the underlying obligations (Bank Bribery Act, FINRA/MSRB gift limits, federal employee gift rules) are structured, threshold-based rules where a deterministic engine is more auditable, cheaper, and more defensible under exam than a language model would be.
  • Two-line structure: the manager approval step (§ 2) is a first-line control — business-line accountability for whether the request makes sense at all. It is intentionally independent of the second-line rules engine (§ 4), which applies the same regulatory thresholds regardless of what the manager decided. A manager's approval does not override a Compliance/Legal escalation; it only clears the request to reach that independent review. A manager's rejection is terminal — the request never reaches second-line review.
  • Thresholds sourced to primary/regulatory text as of 2026-07-08: FINRA Rule 3220 gift limit $300/year (Regulatory Notice 26-05, effective March 30, 2026); federal employee de minimis $20/occasion, $50/year (5 CFR § 2635.204).
  • Several thresholds in this engine are firm-policy overlays, not statutory bright lines firm policy — the Bank Bribery Act and FCPA both turn on corrupt intent rather than a dollar figure. Where this tool applies a specific number (e.g. the $50 foreign-official logging threshold, the $250 general-customer ceiling, the 4-events/year entertainment frequency flag), that number is a configurable internal control choice modeled on common industry practice, not a legal requirement.
  • Rules engine version — tracked on every audit entry, mirroring the model-version-pinning discipline this project applies to AI agents (Layer 4 Governance). A change to any threshold above would be a new version, re-validated before adoption, not a silent edit.
  • Data entered here is stored only for this demonstration session/browser and is not shared. Use "Reset all stored data" to clear it.
