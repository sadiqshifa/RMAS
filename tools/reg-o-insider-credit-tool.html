Methodology & sourcing notes

  • This tool contains no AI. Three of the four Reg O sub-rules encoded here — the board-approval trigger, the executive-officer "other purpose" sub-cap, and the bank-wide aggregate ceiling — are pure arithmetic against a citable dollar or percentage threshold. No language interpretation is involved.
  • Unlike the gifts/entertainment pre-clearance tool, this one has no manager-approval gate. That was a deliberate omission, not an oversight — Reg O already defines its own escalation trigger (the board-approval threshold below), so layering a firm-policy first-line gate on top would add a step the regulation doesn't call for.
  • The executive-officer "other purpose" sub-cap is an absolute prohibition, not an escalation. Unlike the general board-approval trigger, 12 C.F.R. § 215.5(c)(4) does not give the board authority to approve past this cap — the determination reflects that distinction rather than treating every threshold breach the same way.
  • The terms-comparison requirement (§ 215.4(a)) is the one genuine judgment call in this domain and is treated as such: any answer other than "yes" routes to Compliance/Legal regardless of dollar amount, the same pattern used for corrupt-intent red flags in the pre-clearance tool.
  • Thresholds sourced to eCFR primary text as of 2026-07-09: 12 C.F.R. §§ 215.4(a), (b), (d), (e), and 215.5(c)(4).
  • Insider identity now resolves against a fixed 12-person roster rather than free-typed text. This isn't just a convenience — the earlier free-text version had a real bug: "Art Vandelay" and "art vandelay " (trailing space) would have been tracked as two different people with two separate aggregate totals, silently understating actual exposure. Selecting from a roster by a stable ID closes that gap. The "+ Add a new person" option still exists for anyone not yet in the roster, with the same free-text limitation disclosed rather than hidden.
  • Rules engine version , tracked on every audit entry — same version-pinning discipline as the pre-clearance tool and the AI agents elsewhere in this project.
  • Data entered here is stored only for this demonstration session/browser and is not shared. Use "Reset all stored data" to clear it.
